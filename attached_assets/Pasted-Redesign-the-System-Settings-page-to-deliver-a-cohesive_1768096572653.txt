1. **Responsive Architecture**:

   - Replace fixed-width containers with percentage-based or `minmax()` constraints for dynamic resizing.

2. **Unified Navigation & Information Hierarchy**:
   - Restructure the sidebar into a logical, nested taxonomy where primary categories (e.g., Helpdesk, User Management) expand to reveal subcategories (Ticket Settings, SLA Policies) with clear visual grouping.
   - Eliminate redundant top-header toggles by consolidating all related settings under their respective sidebar sections, ensuring one-click access to any configuration.
   - Enforce a "single source of truth" principle: each setting must appear in only one location, with contextual breadcrumbs for navigation clarity.

3. **Department-Specific Configuration**:
   - Overhaul the Helpdesk section to support department-level customization, with a dropdown selector at the top of the section to switch contexts (e.g., IT, HR, Finance).
